About me

Front-end developer from Ukraine, based in Poland

My interest in coding and web development began the summer of 2022 as I was searching for a career which balanced structure and creativity. I discovered the SheCodes workshop through a Facebook group and was immediately intrigued by web development, coding language, and front-end design. Though still in my current role, I began the SheCodes workshops at the beginning of July 2022 and I seek to become a developer soon after completing the course!
In my free time, you can find me playing with my child and doing a home managment, reading, sipping on coffee or wine, enoying the outdoors, cuddled up with my cat, or travelling with my family and taking pictures.
